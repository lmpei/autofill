    <h2>Kalshi is defining a new category</h2>
<p>Kalshi has defined a new category: prediction markets. Kalshi allows people to trade on the outcome of any events and turn any question about the future into a financial asset. Kalshi fought for years and legalized prediction markets in the US for the first time in history. Kalshi is currently the fastest growing financial market in America, and has thousands of markets across politics, economics, financials, weather, tech, AI, culture and more.</p>
<p>We believe prediction markets have the potential to be the largest financial market because they turn anything into a financial position.&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Our vision: well… build the largest financial market on the planet.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Our mission: bring more truth to the world through the power of markets.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p>Building a new category is hard… like really hard. But it’s <a href="https://x.com/Kalshi/status/1853456453886980215">beautiful and deeply fulfilling</a>. Our culture is simple: we hire really talented people, work really hard, and enjoy the climb. We are looking for ambitious and exceptional people to join our (relatively small) team to help us build the next generation of financial markets.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Role Roadmap</strong></span></p>
<p>We are building a next-generation financial ecosystem (think NYSE or CME from scratch). As part of Kalshi, you will work on various platforms, including core exchange, clearinghouse, API infrastructure, connectivity to brokers and market makers, and others. We are a small team, which means your responsibilities scale very rapidly, and your contributions are clear and visible, not marginal. There is still a lot of green field at Kalshi and a lot of it (including entire systems) can be yours.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>What you’ll do</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and implement high-performance backend services and APIs that enable rapid testing of new trading features and experiences as Kalshi’s user-base scales</li>
<li>Architect and maintain systems for personalization engines, recommendation algorithms, and other data-driven features that enhance trader engagement and retention</li>
<li>Partner with product managers and frontend engineers to pilot initiatives that aim to improve or expand Kalshi’s platform</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>What we’re looking for</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>Attributes:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent professional experience, with 4+ years of hands-on software development</li>
<li>Strong command of key programming languages such as Golang, Java, etc.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of REST API design and development</li>
<li>Experience with relational and NoSQL databases, such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB.</li>
<li>Proven track record of taking initiative and thriving in fast-paced environments</li>
<li>Comfortable refining loose requirements into a clear technical spec</li>
<li>Passionate about your craft and committed to delivering exceptional results</li>
</ul>
<h1><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Bonus points</strong></span></h1>
<ul>
<li>Experience supporting low-latency, dynamic interfaces using Websockets or similar event-based infrastructure</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP</li>
<li>Experience with growth experiments or A/B testing frameworks</li>
<li>History with day trading stocks, futures, or options (to better understand the experience of our members)</li>
</ul>
<h2><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Our Culture</strong></span></h2>
<p>Meritocracy is at our core, and we value people who take ownership and figure (usually hard) things out. We dream big. We love our craft deeply and are proud of what we put out in the world. We are committed to our vision of building something big… but also useful: a product that brings more truth through the power of markets.</p>
<p>Kalshians are Kalshi’s most important asset: we pick Kalshians carefully, so we trust them fully on day 1.</p>
<h2><strong>NYC Pay Transparency Disclosure:</strong></h2>
<p>Salary Range: $100,000 to $250,000 annually plus equity and benefits.</p>
<p>This salary range is based on the current available market data and represents the expected salary range for this role. Kalshi has minimal hierarchy and few titles, but a broad range of experience is represented within roles. Should you have compensation expectations that exceed these bands, we'd love to hear from you and would welcome you to reach out to discuss further.</p>
